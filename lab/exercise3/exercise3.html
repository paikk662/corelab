SUNSET LEMONADE
 butterfly blue tea
 lemonade
 strawberry puree
Ingredients: strawberry puree, lemonade, butterfly blue tea.

Grace Street's sunset lemonade is a bright, sweet and tangy drink that looks just as pretty as it tastes. Like its name, the different layers of ingredients creates a gradient that looks like the sunset. It always comes with a big straw and a colorful umbrella. When mixed, it turns into a very pretty magenta color!
